Navjote

Navjote

The initiation ceremony where young Parsis formally join the Zoroastrian faith, typically performed between ages 7-12.

Key Elements:

  • Ritual bath (nahn)
  • Investiture with sacred thread (kusti) and shirt (sudreh)
  • Recitation of prayers
  • Community celebration

Jashan

Jashan

A thanksgiving ceremony performed to mark auspicious occasions, anniversaries, or to invoke divine blessings.

Key Elements:

  • Arrangement of ritual items (ses)
  • Recitation of Avestan prayers
  • Offerings of sandalwood and incense
  • Communal sharing of consecrated fruits and food

Other Sacred Rituals

Fire Ceremonies

Daily and special ceremonies performed around the sacred fire, including the tending of the eternal flame and offerings of sandalwood and incense.

  • Daily fire tending rituals
  • Special occasion prayers
  • Purification ceremonies
  • Blessing rituals

Wedding Ceremonies

Traditional Zoroastrian wedding rituals that unite couples in the presence of the sacred fire and the community, emphasizing spiritual union.

  • Ashirvad (blessing ceremony)
  • Sacred fire witness
  • Exchange of vows
  • Community celebration

Memorial Services

Ceremonies to honor and remember departed souls, including the traditional sky burial practices and memorial prayers.

  • Geh Sarna (prayer periods)
  • Chaharum (fourth day ceremony)
  • Annual memorial prayers
  • Muktad observances
